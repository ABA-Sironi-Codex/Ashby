About the Homeostat:
Inspired by W. Ross Ashby's 1948 Homeostat, this artwork simulates ultrastability. Four interconnected cells are perturbed by the environment. If their stress exceeds the critical threshold, they randomly reorganize their internal synapses until a new state of equilibrium is found. It's a visual metaphor for cybernetic learning and adaptation.
